<p><p>Medical billing outsourcing services can be categorized into three main types: Front End, Middle End, and Back End. The Middle End focuses on coding and charge capture, where services are accurately coded to facilitate payment. The Back End includes claims submission, payment posting, and follow-up on denied claims, ensuring revenue cycle optimization and timely payments for healthcare providers.</p></p>
