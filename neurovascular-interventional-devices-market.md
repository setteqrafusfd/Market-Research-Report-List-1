<p><p>The neurovascular interventional devices market encompasses various types, each serving distinct purposes in treating vascular conditions. Embolization coils are utilized to occlude blood vessels, preventing blood flow to tumors or aneurysms. Carotid stents support narrowed arteries, enhancing blood flow. Intracranial stents are designed for managing intracranial aneurysms. Flow diverters redirect blood flow away from aneurysms. Intrasaccular devices are placed within aneurysms to promote clot formation. Other devices may include thrombectomy tools and balloon catheters, expanding treatment options.</p></p>
