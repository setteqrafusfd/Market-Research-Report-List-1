<p><p>The spinal traction market comprises two primary types: manual and mechanical traction. Manual spinal traction involves a therapist using hands-on techniques to apply force, aiding in spinal alignment and relieving pressure on nerves and discs. In contrast, mechanical spinal traction employs devices or machines to deliver controlled pulling force, providing a consistent and adjustable treatment option. Both methods aim to alleviate back pain, improve mobility, and promote healing in spinal conditions, appealing to patients seeking non-invasive therapy solutions.</p></p>
