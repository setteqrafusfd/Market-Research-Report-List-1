<p><p>The tie bars market encompasses two main types: two-platen tie bars and three-platen tie bars. Two-platen tie bars are designed for simplicity and cost-effectiveness, typically used in low to mid-range injection molding applications, allowing for efficient clamping and greater space savings. In contrast, three-platen tie bars are engineered for high precision and reduced cycle times, ideal for complex molds and larger part production. The choice between these types depends on production needs, mold designs, and operational efficiencies.</p></p>
