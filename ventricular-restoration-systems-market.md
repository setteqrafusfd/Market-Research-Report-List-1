<p><p>The ventricular restoration systems market encompasses techniques aimed at improving cardiac function by reshaping and restoring the heart's ventricles. Percutaneous ventricular restoration involves minimally invasive procedures, utilizing catheters to repair or remodel ventricular structures without major surgery. In contrast, surgical ventricular restoration entails open-heart surgeries that directly correct ventricular shape and function through techniques like resection or suturing.
